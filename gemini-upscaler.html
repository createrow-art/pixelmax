Frequently Asked Questions

What resolution do Gemini AI images output?

Google Gemini generates images at 1024×1024 pixels by default. Imagen 3 can output slightly higher resolution, but all outputs are designed for web viewing, not printing. At 300 DPI, 1024px gives you only 3.4 inches of print size.

Can I upscale Gemini images for Etsy?

Absolutely. Many Etsy digital download sellers use PixelMax to upscale Gemini-generated art to print-ready resolution. After 4× upscaling, your 1024px image becomes 4096px — suitable for 13.6-inch prints at 300 DPI.

How does PixelMax compare to Topaz Gigapixel?

Topaz Gigapixel is a desktop app costing $199/year that requires a powerful GPU. PixelMax runs in the cloud, costs $1 per image, and works in any browser. Both use AI upscaling, but PixelMax is dramatically simpler and cheaper.

Will upscaling add artifacts to my image?

Real-ESRGAN is specifically designed to avoid artifacts during upscaling. It reconstructs detail intelligently rather than simply stretching pixels. The result is a sharp, clean image at 4× the original resolution.

How long does it take to upscale a Gemini image?

Most images are processed in about 30 seconds. Upload, select your scale factor, and download — it's that simple.
